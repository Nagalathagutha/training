HTC 11's alleged specifications surface online, claim 8GB RAM



NEW DELHI:eems the upcoming HTC flagship, HTC 11, will be a 'monster smartphone'. According to a report in Timesnews.com website, the upcoming HTC 11 will bring back the dual-rear camera setup, which it introduced with the HTC One M8 (Eye).

The handset is also said to be powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 835 processor built using 10nm process, along with the Adreno 540 GPU. Powering the device will be a massive 8GB RAM.

As for the display, the HTC 11 might sport a 5.5-inch QHD (1440 x 2560 pixels) resolution LCD display. In addition to 256GB inbuilt storage, the smartphone will feature a 12MP rear dual camera along with an 8MP front-facing camera.
