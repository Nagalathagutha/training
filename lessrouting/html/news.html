NEW DELHI: India will be without Ajinkya Rahane for the fourth and fifth Test against England,the BCCI has announced, with uncapped Karnataka batsman Manish Pandey called up for the first time to the Test squad. that Mumbai pace bowler Shardul Thakur has been added to the squad for the fourth Test in Mumbai as cover for Mohammed Shami, who played through the Mohali match with a sore knee.
In a press release on Wednesday, the BCCI clarified that Rahane had been struck on the right index finger during practice and "sustained an avulsion fracture" and that he would play no further role in the series. The fourth Test starts Thursday at the Mumbai's Wankhede Stadium and the final in Chennai from December 16.
