Ruble hits 17-month high vs dollar on oil cut deal



MOSCOW: The ruble hit its highest level in 17 months against the dollar Monday as oil prices surged following a landmark deal by Russia and other non-OPEC producers to join the cartel in capping output.
In morning trading, the dollar traded at 61.05 rubles, a level not seen since October 2015, and the euro at 64.54 rubles, also a 17-month high.
