Alert Amazon techie outfoxes cyber crooks



HYDERABAD: An alert software engineer managed to outfox a cyber crook posing as a bank employee who tried to siphon off Rs 19,400 from his account on Sunday. City-based Amazon employee Jashnil Bodele received a call from a fraudster posing as an SBI employee around 1.30pm on Sunday. "My SBI debit card had expired some time ago and the telecaller, posing as an SBI employee, asked me whether I want to get a fresh card. I said yes. He then asked me to provide an alternate debit card particular as a guarantor card," Jashnil told TOI.
